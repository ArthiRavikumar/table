Steps to Make a Cup of Tea (Numbered List)

  1. Boil water in a kettle.
  2. Add tea leaves or a tea bag to a cup.
  3. Pour the hot water into the cup.
  4. Let it steep for 3-5 minutes.
  5. Add sugar or milk as per taste and enjoy.
